About Millie

I was excited to contribute to the world knowing this would be
the type of book men and women, young or adult could relate
to and gain the knowledge that they are not alone. To know
that they were not the only ones who have been sexual
assaulted or raped.

It all started when her dad retired from the police
force and moved to a small village outside of Accra,
Ghana. As a kid who didn't know any better it was there
where she was raped by a church priest, strangers, friends
and even family members. She believed the whole world was
against her.

She became homeless when her sister and her husband kicked
her out the house. She survived by having faith, hope,
and believing in the only person who she could rely on
was God. Even back then she knew there was a light at the
end of the tunnel.
